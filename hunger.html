Hunger
Description
"Where do you want to eat?" ...  "What are you in the mood for?"
Hunger is an iOS app that solves these familiar questions. Users can select a variety of food types, view photos of dozens of dishes, and have tables booked at nearby restaurants: all in a matter of seconds.
Technologies
React Native,  Google Firebase,  GoogleMaps,  GooglePlaces
